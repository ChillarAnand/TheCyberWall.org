About The Cyber Wall

Countless devices(servers, ip cameras, IoT devices, medical equipments) which are connected to internet are vulnerable to several attacks.

The Cyber Wall aims to fix all these vulnerabilites to make devices more secure and trustworthy.

Scanner

We are building a completely free vulnerability scanner to scan for hundrends of vulnerabilites and show a detailed report on the impact and remedies for these vulnerabilites.
